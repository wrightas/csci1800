CSCI1800, Computing and Society in History

Lecture Slides and Course Info

Welcome! This page provides access to the HTML slides for CSCI1800, taught by Dr. Aaron S. Wright at Dalhousie University. For Teaching Assistants, assignments, discussion boards, grades, and more, log on to the course BrightSpace page.

The slides are produced in HTML and JavaScript with reveal.js. Here is a demonstration of how you can interact with the slides. Reveal.js is an open source project.

One of my goals in making HTML slides is to achieve good accessibility. This includes compatibility with assitive technology like screen readers and also the ability to use screen translators, or view slides at your own pace. Please get in touch if you have any comments or questions about this.

Video presentations of the slides will be posted on BrightSpace / Panopto. The slides posted here have the same content as the slides in the video.

CSCI1800 is divided into modules. After the one-week “Module 0: Course Introduction,” the remaining modules last three weeks. Each module requires students to watch video slide presentations, complete the assigned readings, and complete a set of writing assignments leading up to a short essay. See the course syllabus for more details.
